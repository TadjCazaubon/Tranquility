Terms of Service

The following terms of use are in place.

  1. The lead guest must be at least 18 years old at the time of check in.
  2. To secure or confirm your booking, a deposit must be paid equal to 50% of your entire stay.
  3. Guests must check out before 12 noon and return their key to the office. If you require a late checkout, please ask reception if this can be accommodated. Late checkouts require an additional $50.00 EC be paid for the extra time and can only be extended up to 4 pm.
  4. Cancellations are allowed without penalty up to 48 hours before check-in time. Any cancellations made after this time will be charged 50% of the original booking plus all applicable taxes.
  5. Tranquility Getaway Ltd. reserves the right to cancel your booking without compensation, refund or reimbursement if any of these conditions are not upheld.
  6. Failure to show up or non-arrival of guests without communication or cancellation, forfeits any and all deposits made.
  7. Refunds: will be issued to guests within 30 days where applicable in the same manner in which the payment was made.
  8. Damages: We reserve the right to charge the lead guest for any damages caused throughout the course of your stay by any member of your party. This includes breakages, stains and damage to furniture, damage to fixtures and missing items. Any accidental breakage must be reported immediately to the office staff or the security guard on duty in an effort to avoid this charge.
  9. Lost keys will be charged a replacement fee of $20.00 xcd.
  10. Smoking is prohibited in the rooms and will incur a charge of $250.00 xcd to be paid by the lead guest. Smoking can be done in the verandas or any of the open spaces on property.
  11. Absolutely No pets are allowed on property.
  12. Guests parking in the on property parking lot must first accept that they park at their own risk.
